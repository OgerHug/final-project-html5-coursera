About the Course and Instructor
Course Title: Ordinary Differential Equations

Welcome to the Differential Equations Basics Course! Throughout this program, we'll explore essential concepts like first-order and nth-order linear equations, systems of linear differential equations, and Laplace transforms. By course end, you'll proficiently recognize and solve various differential equations, interpret solutions accurately, and apply them to real-world engineering and scientific challenges. We'll also integrate modern technology for enhanced learning experiences. Join us as we align our studies with Sustainable Development Goal 4, shaping a brighter future through the power of mathematics!

Throughout this course, you'll engage in three modules, each comprising formative and summative tasks.

  • Module 1 focuses on solving first-order differential equations, exploring analytic solutions and the method of auxiliary equations.
  • Module 2, delves into higher-order differential equations, along with common models and word problems.
  • Module 3 introduces the powerful method of Laplace Transforms for tackling initial value problems.
    • Your performance in each module will contribute to your module class standing, culminating in a final class standing computed from these individual standings.Prepare to immerse yourself fully in this comprehensive exploration of differential equations!
